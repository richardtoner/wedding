Location

Our ceremony & reception wll be held at Rosedew Farm in Llantwit Major, Wales. The venue is a short walk from the beautiful South Wales coast.

Address:
Rosedew Farm, Beach Road, Llantwit Major, Vale of Glamorgan, Wales, CF61 1PZ

Getting Here:
Driving:
From Cardiff the journey is around 40 minutes.
There is plenty of parking at the venue and you are welcome to leave your car overnight and pick it up the following morning.

Train:
Llantwit Major station (40 minute train from Cardiff Central) is a 5 minute taxi ride, or 20 minute walk, to Rosedew Farm
